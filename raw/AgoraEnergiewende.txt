agora energiewende agora energiewende is a think tank supporting the energiewende in germany it is funded by stiftung mercator de and the european climate foundation  its members debate under the chatham house rule and include klaus töpfer ottmar edenhofer and claude turmes in addition to studies specific to germany agora have published an analysis on the economics of the proposed uk hinkley point c nuclear power plant  its analyses are quoted as part of the energy debate in other countries including denmark
